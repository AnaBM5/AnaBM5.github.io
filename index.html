About Me

I'm a software engineer with a passion for precision and a knack for creating immersive experiences. With a Game Development diploma and extensive experience in Unity, as well as experience working with languages such as C++, C# and Java.

I've spent years honing my craft, transforming abstract ideas into engaging, interactive worlds. My learning journey being driven by a deep-seated love for storytelling through technology, and my work being a testament to my commitment to detail and my belief that every pixel, every line of code, tells a part of a larger story.

I hope you enjoy exploring the projects that I have done for both PC and mobile devices, and that the care that I put into my work becomes evident. While I strive to push boundaries and challenge conventions, I also willingly embrace every opportunity to learn and grow, continually seeking ways to improve and create even better experiences.

Highlighted Projects

Taco Tour

Taco Tour is an engaging endless runner game developed with Unity. In this game, players must switch between Taco and Tour to navigate a series of challenging obstacles. As players progress, they can collect power-ups that enhance their gameplay experience. With vibrant graphics, intuitive controls, and an exciting array of obstacles and power-ups, Taco Tour offers endless fun and challenge for players of all ages.

Goofy Groovers

GoofyGroovers is a platformer racing game developed with Monogame. It invites you to a multiplayer pixel art experience where you and your friends compete in a race. Use the mouse to determine where yout blob is going to jump to, and stick your way through the walls of the map to arrive first to the finish line!

Catsper

Catsper is a memory game made in Unity where you help find the souls of pets that have passed away and reunite them with their owners. You play as Diggory, a retired ghost hunter who, after losing his only friend Sussie, decides to help others in similar situations. Now, you have to remember the details of the room as you make your way through haunted houses to find the place where the pet's soul resides.
